WELCOME TO GETARE SDA ACADEMY

This is a premier school located in Kisii county, using the Kisii town-Marani highway it is located along Geturi Nyabonge area they are sharing the fence with NYABONGE SDA CHURCH. The school was established in the year 2002 by the community together with the church service atleast to create a study area for the growing generation.

Initially it was referred to as Nyabonge School before being renamed to GETARE SDA ACADEMY by the community.
